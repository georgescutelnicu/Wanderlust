ABOUT

Wanderlust is a comprehensive Travel Destination Platform that combines a user-friendly website and a powerful RESTful API. The website offers an interactive user-friendly interface where you can discover travel destinations, view detailed city information, check real-time weather updates and booking flight tikets. Simultaneously, the RESTful API empowers developers to programmatically access data, enabling them to retrieve, create, update, and delete destinations.

Discover New Destinations

Wanderlust lets you dive into cities around the world with detailed and structured data. Each destination offers ratings on categories like food, nightlife, sightseeing, hiking, beaches, shopping, museums, and more, helping you understand what each place truly offers. You’ll also get a real-time weather forecast and an integrated flight ticket booking widget to plan and book your trip confidently. Whether you're exploring urban hubs or nature escapes, Wanderlust gives you the tools to compare, evaluate, and choose your next journey with ease.

Save Your Spots

Create a personal Wanderlust account to save destinations to your "Visited" and "Plan to Visit" lists. With these features, you can easily organize your travel plans and keep track of where you’ve been or where you want to go next. Use the interactive map to visualize your journey, set future travel goals, and build a detailed travel journal. This makes it simple to revisit your favorite destinations and plan new adventures, all in one place, just a click away.

Tap into the API

Wanderlust offers more than just a travel website, developers can access a powerful RESTful API to interact with the platform’s data. The API enables retrieving detailed destination info and real-time weather, as well as adding and updating destinations directly on the platform keeping the travel content fresh and up-to-date. Fully documented and secured with API keys, it’s perfect for creating custom travel apps, research projects, or enhancing existing services with travel data. Jump in now to get your API key and start exploring!
